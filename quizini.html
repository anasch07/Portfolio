Quizini - Graph-Based Quiz Platform

Description

  • Anas Chaibi | Omar Besbes | Med Mongi Saidene

  • July 2023 - September 2023

Quizini stands as a dynamic educational platform where professors craft interactive, graph-based quizzes directly within the system, offering students a visually engaging way to deepen their understanding of complex topics. Beyond its innovative quiz creation capabilities, Quizini boasts comprehensive statistics and tracking features, allowing students to monitor their performance and progress.

Educators benefit from these insights to fine-tune teaching strategies and offer personalized feedback.

By blending custom-made educational content with powerful analytics, Quizini enriches the learning experience, promoting both analytical thinking and self-improvement in an intuitive, user-centric environment.
